About HopeConnect

HopeConnect is a community donation platform designed to connect donors with verified NGOs and volunteers, making the donation process simple, transparent, and efficient.

Our Mission

To reduce food waste and encourage the donation of essential items by providing a reliable platform that connects donors with organizations serving people in need.

Our Vision

To build a caring community where surplus resources are shared responsibly, ensuring that no usable food or essential item goes to waste.
